Aircraft Search

Search for aircraft according to your interest. By entering the minimum and maximum values, you will see a list of aircraft within your specified price range, and you will be able to compare them more easily.

Complete Calculator

Now you can enter information about a certain route and receive a more complete suggestion, such as: seat configuration, flight time according to the cost index, profit, price and more...

Trips/day

Find out how many flights per day it is possible to make on a given route and with a certain number of planes. In addition to giving you the result, the calculator will inform the ideal number of planes that will be able to operate on that route, just so you don't have problems with daily demand.

Seats Configuration

Plug in First Class and Business demands to get the highest profit seat configuration - priority given to First Class, then Business, remaining seats go to Economy (no need to enter Economy demand).

Ticket Calculator

Calculate the ideal ticket price for your route!

Profit Calculator

Plug in all the required values to get an accurate per day profit estimate for your route!
